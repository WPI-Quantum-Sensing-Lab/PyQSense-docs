class PyQSense.gui.Camera_Thread(*args: Any, **kwargs: Any)#

Bases: QThread

Grab images and check for new camera configuration.

On run, it sets the camera GenICam parameters and takes an image. The image is sent through a Qt signal.

update_image#

alias of ndarray

__init__()#

Initialize self.params and self.running flag.

run()#

Check for new configuration and take image.

Checks if the camera parameters have been updated, and if so, notifies the camera and reprograms the TTL pulse sequence to match the desired exposure time (GenICam: “ExposureTime”) and readout time (GenICam: “SensorReadoutTime”) A camera image is always taken, and the image is emitted through a Qt signal.

update_params(params)#

Change the camera parameters.

Parameters:

params (dict) – The GenICam parameters to set.

update_ttl(readout_delay, exposure_time)#

Update TTL pulse sequence to match camera parameters.

The pulse sequence is reprogrammed to match the requested exposure time (GenICam: “ExposureTime”) and readout delay (GenICam: “SensorReadoutTime”).

stop()#

Stop the thread.
